About me

A picture of me scubadiving

I'm Andreas, from Kristianstad with a strong interest in science and technology. As a child I spent hours building with lego, figuring out how to create structures and models. In secondary school, I studied IT-Media, which gave me a chance to explore programming and digitaldesign. Today, I'm working toward a career in a medical field where I can apply scientific knowledge in a hands-on way. While I'm deeply interested in chemistry and how it relates to health, I also enjoy programming as a creative outlet. I enjoy the process of taking an idea and turning it into something functional, even if it is just a hobby. I balance my studies with visiting museums and traveling whenever I get the chance.

Future goals

My primary goal is to work in a medical field. This goal matters to me because I want to be part of work that genuinely helps people and makes a difference in their health and everyday lives. I have always enjoyed working with my hands, and I find it rewarding when I can do something practical and see the results of my effort. That is one of the reasons a medical career feels like the right path for me. At the same time, I want programming to stay a meaningful hobby in my life. I enjoy the creativity it gives me, and also how I can sit down, focus on a problem and slowly build a solution in my own way.
